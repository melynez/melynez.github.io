About

Hi! I’m a 16 y/o exploring all things bioelectricity and diverse intelligence.

I’m currently working with Dr. Michael Levin investigating complexity algorithms. Previously, I’ve worked in labs from materials science to organoids for cancer.

I’m interested in investigating the psychology of unconventional systems—i.e. applying behavioral tests typically administered in mice to deceptively simple organisms, algorithms, and synthetic/bioengineered systems. Fascinated by “code” in biology, such as deciphering patterns of communication between cells in the brain and elsewhere in the body.

I’m a fast learner, and I love diving down rabbit holes. Recent deep dives include organoids for cancer, biobots (anthrobots, neurobots), and developmental robotics & zero-shot learning. Curious about systems for innovation and scientific progress.
